About

Hi, my name is Stephen

I am a slightly sarcastic, mildly entertaining, and extremely passionate brand and experience designer. I believe good user-experience and design, across all types of media, has the power to connect people and create communities.

Outside of work, I spend time pondering how to get on top of large rocks, digging pits in the snow, brewing beer, doodling, and balancing on two wheels. When I’m not doing any of these, I enjoy meeting people through beer, music, and my grumpy dog, Jeff.
