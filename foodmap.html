Boulder Food Map

As a strong advocate for helping those within my community as much as personally possible, I became interested in what resources Boulder County had for vulnerable communities in need of food. It was simple to find resources using the web, but for those without access to the internet, the resources were hard to come by.

In an attempt to connect the relevant resources to a larger percentage of the in-need community, I worked with a friend to create and distribute an informational poster that contained a straightforward map and details on various available resources.
